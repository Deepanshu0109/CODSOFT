Our Must Try Dishes!

Our North Indian Thali brings together the soul of Indian cuisine in one wholesome platter. It features aromatic rice, soft chapatis, creamy dal, spiced vegetables, tangy chutneys, and a touch of sweetness on the side. Each item is prepared using authentic spices and traditional cooking methods. It’s a hearty, balanced meal perfect for anyone wanting a complete taste of India.

This golden, crispy dosa is thin yet filling, rolled to perfection and served with classic sambar and chutney accompaniments. The light rice-lentil crepe delivers a satisfying crunch while its stuffing adds warmth and flavor. Whether you're familiar with South Indian food or trying it for the first time, this dish guarantees a comforting experience. It's the kind of simplicity that leaves a lasting impression.

Our Manchurian is a bold, flavorful blend of Indo-Chinese fusion — crispy vegetable balls deep-fried and tossed in a spicy garlic-soy sauce. Each bite bursts with tang, spice, and umami, making it an irresistible appetizer or side. The glossy coating and fresh garnishing elevate its visual appeal and taste alike. It’s the perfect choice when you want something crunchy, saucy, and unforgettable.

This towering burger is a treat for both the eyes and the appetite — packed with layers of crisp lettuce, juicy tomato, onion, and a thick patty inside a sesame-seed bun. Every element, from the fresh veggies to the savory patty, contributes to a rich, satisfying bite. Our house-made sauces bring it all together with just the right zing. It’s a fast food classic done with flavor and freshness.

Delicious penne pasta coated in a rich tomato mascarpone sauce and garnished with fresh herbs and cheese — this dish brings a creamy, tangy twist to your plate. The sauce balances spice and sweetness while the pasta is cooked al dente for perfect texture. It’s comforting, hearty, and ideal for those who love Italian food with a flavorful Indian flair. One bite and you’ll come back for more.

This chilled coffee is a creamy, indulgent treat — made with freshly brewed espresso, cold milk, ice, and topped with frothy cream and chocolate drizzle. It's rich but smooth, sweet but balanced, and energizing without being heavy. Whether you need a mid-day boost or a cool companion to your meal, this iced coffee is a café-style refreshment worth savoring. It's not just a drink — it’s an experience.
